Being a student

When I got to high school I was thrilled! I thought I was finally going to learn more about the magical world of computing. However it wasn’t until the 3rth year I actually got thought IT. The lesson consisted of our teacher explaining how a processor worked and why there was RAM in a computer all the basic stuff. Ever since then I strife to learn as much as I possibly can on these subjects


Currently I’m studying my 2nd year of Software Developing where I’m soon to have an intership in the world where I’ve been wanting to be part of for so long. Internship

Alright let’s get to business! Currently I’m on a search for an internship this is a required stage in my study however I want to turn it up a notch.


Because America has always intrigued me it’s become a dream to eventually live and work in the United States, therefore I’m actively searching for an Internship abroad that offers me an American work experience. Not only would living in America for this period be amazing but being introduced into the American business world is the real reason behind this grand plan.


To go into specifics my Intern period will be during a 6 month period. In this period it is required of me to work for 20 weeks at the given employer. Besides that my school requires me to work on a project for 16 weeks. This project has to be orientated around Software Development and take up the majority of the intern period. The rest of these weeks are reserved for getting to know your co-workers and getting acquainted with the working environment.


If you are stationed in the Los Angeles region / California region and you are in need of an intern I’d love to have a chat. You can contact me via e-mail where we can plan a skype conversation.
